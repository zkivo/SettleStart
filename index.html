An opening-placement analysis tool for Catan-like board games

Click a tile/token to change terrain/number.

What are pips?
Pips are the dots under the tokens. Each dot represents a unique combination of dice used to make up that token number. Therefore, the more dots (pips), the more valuable the resource is.
Between the tiles, at the vertices of the resources, the sum of the adjacent pips are reported. These sum express the strength of a settlement placed at that point.

SettleStart is an unofficial, fan-made analysis tool. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or associated with the publishers of Catan.
